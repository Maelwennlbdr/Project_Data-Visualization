Lab: Decision Tree and Random Forest on Wisconsin Breast Dataset

Number of Instances: 569 Number of Features: 30 (plus the target column)
Features:
All features are real-valued, with values representing various characteristics of cell nuclei such as radius, texture, perimeter, area, smoothness, etc.
Some key feature categories:

  • Radius: Mean of distances from center to points on the perimeter
  • Texture: Standard deviation of gray-scale values
  • Perimeter: Size of the perimeter of the nuclei
  • Area: The area of the nuclei
  • Smoothness: Variation in the smoothness of the cell perimeter
  • Compactness, concavity, and symmetry: These also contribute to the characterization of nuclei.

Target Labels:

  • Malignant (M): Cancerous tumor
  • Benign (B): Non-cancerous tumor

Summary Statistics:

  • Benign Cases: 357
  • Malignant Cases: 212
